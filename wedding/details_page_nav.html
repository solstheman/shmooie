The Original Plan
We had planned a beautiful ceremony at the Fontainebleau Inn on Labor Day weekend. It was the whole 9 yards; we're talking the Hora, the speeches, the cake!
But...there was a global pandemic.

So we dismantled our plan.

Now 23 people will join us for our Mini Matrimony. Same time, same place...just a little different than expected.

We are so excited to have a live stream for those who can't join us. But that's not enough! As soon as it's safe, we will have a big party. Stay tuned!
